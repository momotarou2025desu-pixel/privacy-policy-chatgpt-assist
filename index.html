Privacy Policy

App: AI Fast Assist Vol.1
Last updated: 2026-02-13

Summary: This app stores notes, lists, and settings you enter locally on your device for app functionality. The app may display ads and may offer a one-time in-app purchase (for example, to remove ads).

1. Information We Handle

Depending on how you use the app, we may handle the following types of information:

  • User-provided content (local storage): notes, lists, and settings you create in the app. This content is stored in the app’s local storage on your device.
  • Purchase-related information: purchase status (for example, whether the one-time purchase is owned). Payment details (such as credit card numbers) are processed by Google Play and are not collected by us.
  • Advertising-related information (when ads are enabled): an advertising SDK may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), IP address, device/app information, approximate location, and ad performance data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.
  • App diagnostics: minimal technical information used for troubleshooting (shown on-device, or shared externally only if you choose to share it).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core features (create, display, edit, and delete notes/lists and manage settings).
  • To deliver and measure ads, control frequency, and prevent fraud (when ads are enabled).
  • To provide and verify the one-time purchase (e.g., ad removal) via Google Play.
  • To diagnose issues and improve the app.

3. Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not send your note contents to external servers unless you explicitly use a sharing/export feature.

However, the following third-party services may receive certain information as needed to provide their services:

  • Google Play (Billing): processes purchases and helps verify purchase status.
  • Advertising providers (Ads): deliver ads, measure performance, and help prevent fraud (when ads are enabled).

4. Ads

The app may show ads. If ads are enabled, ad SDKs may collect information to deliver ads and to measure and prevent fraud. You may be able to control personalized ads via your device and Google settings (availability depends on your device and region).

5. One-Time In-App Purchase

The app may offer a one-time in-app purchase (for example, to remove ads). Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not store your payment information. The app may check your purchase status to enable purchased features.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Your notes and settings are stored locally in the app’s storage on your device. They remain there until you delete them in the app or remove the app / clear its data via your device settings.

7. Your Controls

  • You can delete notes and other saved content from within the app.
  • You can clear the app’s data or uninstall the app using your device settings.
  • You may be able to reset your Advertising ID and limit personalized ads in device settings.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored by the app. However, no method of storage is perfectly secure, and risks (e.g., device loss or unauthorized access) cannot be fully eliminated.

9. Children

If you are under the age of majority in your jurisdiction, please use the app with the consent of a parent or guardian.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time (for example, when adding or changing ads, billing, or other SDKs). Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
